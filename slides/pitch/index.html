The Problem

Biology is trapped in PDFs. That has to change.

📄
Knowledge is disconnected
Papers describe methods, but the code, data, parameters, and validation are scattered. Biology is hard to reproduce for humans — and almost impossible for AI agents.
🔒
Sensitive data cannot leave
Health and genomic data must stay on your own device. Cloud-only solutions are a non-starter for clinical and personal genomics workflows.
🤖
No infrastructure layer
AI agents have no standardised way to call biological methods. Every lab reinvents the wheel. The foundational execution layer was missing.
ClawBio turns biomedical methods into reusable, verifiable, local-first, agent-native skills.
Instead of static papers, you get workflows that actually run.
