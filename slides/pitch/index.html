The Problem

Three unsolved problems. One platform.

🤖
AI agents cannot call biology
No standardised way to call a genomic method. Every lab reinvents the wheel. The infrastructure layer was missing.
Corpas et al. (under review, Comms Biology)
🔗
1.5 billion people are excluded
Biobanks, clinical trials, and literature systematically exclude the world's most vulnerable populations — by design.
Corpas et al., medRxiv 2026 · doi:10.64898/2026.02.10.26346004
📄
LLMs hallucinate biology
Empirically unreliable for clinical or genomic decisions. You cannot trust them without validated, auditable methods underneath.
Corpas & Iacoangeli (in revision, PLOS Computational Biology) · researchsquare.com/article/rs-6098960/v1
